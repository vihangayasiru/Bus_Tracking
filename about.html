About This Website

This website helps commuters find bus schedules, track buses in real-time, and soon, book tickets online. Focused on improving transport services in Sri Lanka.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track a bus?

Visit the "Bus Tracking System" page from the navigation bar to see live bus locations on a map.

Can I book tickets online?

Online booking is coming soon. Stay tuned for updates on our booking page.

How accurate is the bus timetable?

We update our timetable regularly based on real-time data from the transport department.

Is the tracking feature available on mobile?

Yes! Our website is mobile-friendly and works across devices.
